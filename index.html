Hi, I'm Vivian!

The question “Who are you?” has always made me pause, not because I don’t know, but because I’ve never been just one thing. I love storytelling through writing, creating art like sketches and nail designs, and building things through code. I’m drawn to both imagination and structure, creativity and technology. For a long time, I wasn’t sure how it all connected.

Then came mediocremango, and slowly, the answer started to take shape. So… what is a mediocremango?

What's a mediocremango?

↓

mediocremango /ˌmē-dē-ˈō-krə-ˌmaŋ-gō/ (n.)

1. A digital personification of Vivian Park. What began as a video game username coined at age 15 became her go-to persona on nearly every platform—and eventually, a part of her identity.

2. A thematic umbrella for Vivian’s creative pursuits: mangoblog (a space for writing and storytelling), mangonails (a visual diary of experimental nail art), and mangoprojects (a home for code, design, and things she builds).

Origin: A blend of “mediocre” (a playful nod to imperfection) and “mango” (her favorite fruit), brought together with a love for alliteration.

Synonyms: digital diary, fruit-flavored identity
